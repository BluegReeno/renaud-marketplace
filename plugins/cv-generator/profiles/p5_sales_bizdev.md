# P5 — Sales / BizDev

**Definition**: Pure commercial role — AE, BDM. Distinct from CS: here you hunt and close, delivery is secondary. ~5 applications. 2 cells.

## Valid cells: 2 company types ONLY

- P5×T3 — ESN / Consulting (ILLUIN Senior AE — active process, stage 2/4)
- P5×T5 — AI SaaS / Lab (Anthropic AE, Mistral BDM, Fennex)

## CRITICAL: P5×T5 experience ORDER

For P5×T5, the experience order in the CV MUST be: **Artelia → Blue Green → Open Ocean**

This is the corporate_first rule. The Anthropic rejection was explicit about the solopreneur profile. The narrative must open with Artelia (corporate quota-carrying, +15% YOY) and Open Ocean (8 years of enterprise cycles), with Blue Green last as product-mastery proof.

NEVER lead with Blue Green or "founder" for P5×T5.

## Narrative rules

**P5×T3 (ESN)**: The "selling architect" — technical enough to scope the bid with the technical team. This shortens sales cycles and is what ESNs value.
- Proof: Open Ocean (prospecting → C-level closing, 8 years), Artelia (+15% YOY, complex enterprise contracts)
- He runs demos himself and can scope the AO with the technical team

**P5×T5 (AI SaaS/Lab)**: Builds with the product he sells. Uses Claude/Mistral in production daily.
- Energy/industry network (15 years, FR-UK-BR): opens verticals generalist sellers can't reach
- Live demos > slides: actual product knowledge from daily use

## Anti-patterns

- Never open P5×T5 on "founder" or "indépendant"
- Never lead P5×T5 with Blue Green (reads as "small consulting shop")
- Never hide the technical fluency — for AI SaaS, it IS a differentiator

## Solopreneur neutralisation (P5×T5 only)

Experience order: Artelia → Blue Green → Open Ocean (corporate-first)
- Artelia: quota-carrying corporate sales (+15% YOY on offshore portfolio, London)
- Open Ocean: 8 years B2B full sales cycles at scale (prospecting → C-level closing, €2M raised)
- Blue Green last: product-mastery proof (daily user of frontier models, live production systems)

## Key differentiators

- Technical enough to run live demos and scope solutions with engineering teams
- Energy/industry vertical network — opens accounts generalist AEs can't
- 20 years of sales experience in technical services (not just AI)
