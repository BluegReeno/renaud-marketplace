# P4 — Customer Success / Forward Deployed Engineer

**Definition**: Ensure the sold solution answers the client need + successful integration. More commercial than the architect, but delivery-oriented (not P5 Sales). 2 cells.

## Valid cells: 2 company types ONLY

- P4×T1 — Industrial startup (Oplit FDE)
- P4×T5 — AI SaaS / Lab (Dust SE — active pipeline, OpenAI Solution Eng, Nabla, Zaion)

## Narrative rules

**Order: technical → delivery → enterprise empathy**. The About must lead with what he *builds*, not what he *sells*.

**Technical anchor**: HAL (27-tool MCP platform, Claude Code marketplace install) + BlueWind Companion (5 agents, FR+UK regulatory corpus, 91 docs → 22,812 pgvector chunks, benchmarks 8.6/10) — real systems in production.

**Deployment history is the delivery anchor**: DCNS, SBM Offshore, RTE (Open Ocean), then EnBW France + Valorem (Blue Green). Proof he can deploy with industrial clients from discovery to autonomous production use.

**Three-legs FDE model** (especially for T5):
1. Technical: builds and ships — HAL MCP platform (27 tools, marketplace install), BlueWind Companion multi-agent system in production
2. Delivery: demos, POCs, handover — full cycles at Open Ocean and Blue Green with named industrial clients
3. Enterprise empathy: 4 years inside Artelia's IT division — cloud selection committees (AWS/GCP/Azure), IS governance, AI adoption friction seen from the inside

**T1 FDE focus**: win the trust of sceptical operations people, adapt the product to the field. Language: "I deploy, not just consult."

**T5 anti-pattern**: Neutralise the solopreneur gap. Frame Blue Green as multi-client delivery (named clients). Stress the product teamwork aspect of Open Ocean.

## Solopreneur neutralisation

- Blue Green: multi-client AI studio (EnBW, Valorem, IC Ingénieurs) — never "consulting"
- Open Ocean: 12-person multidisciplinary team — hiring, roadmap, delivery at scale

## Key differentiators

- 15+ years of energy/infrastructure sector expertise — not framed as "client side" but as domain depth
- Full cycle ownership: discovery → demo → POC → production handover
- Leaves customers autonomous: doesn't just deploy, he trains and onboards
- Enterprise-insider knowledge grounded in 4 real years in Artelia's IT division (governance, selection processes, adoption friction) — not a generic claim
