# P4 — Customer Success / Forward Deployed Engineer

**Definition**: Ensure the sold solution answers the client need + successful integration. More commercial than the architect, but delivery-oriented (not P5 Sales). 2 cells.

## Valid cells: 2 company types ONLY

- P4×T1 — Industrial startup (Oplit FDE)
- P4×T5 — AI SaaS / Lab (Dust SE — active pipeline, OpenAI Solution Eng, Nabla, Zaion)

## Narrative rules

**Deployment history is the credibility anchor**: DCNS, SBM Offshore, RTE (Open Ocean), then EnBW France + Valorem (Blue Green). This is the proof that he can deploy with industrial clients, not just sell to them.

**Three-legs FDE model** (especially for T5):
1. Technical: builds with the API — BWC = Claude multi-agent in production
2. Commercial: demos, POCs, closing — 100% of cycles at Open Ocean (8 years)
3. Enterprise empathy: 15 years on the industrial-client side

**T1 FDE focus**: win the trust of sceptical operations people, adapt the product to the field. Language: "I deploy, not just consult."

**T5 anti-pattern**: Neutralise the solopreneur gap. Frame Blue Green as multi-client delivery (named clients). Stress the product teamwork aspect of Open Ocean.

## Solopreneur neutralisation

- Blue Green: multi-client AI studio (EnBW, Valorem, IC Ingénieurs) — never "consulting"
- Open Ocean: 12-person multidisciplinary team — hiring, roadmap, delivery at scale
- "Has literally been the user-buyer that GenAI vendors target"

## Key differentiators

- Was the customer before being the FDE: 15 years on the industrial-client side
- Full cycle ownership: discovery → demo → POC → production handover
- Leaves customers autonomous: doesn't just deploy, he trains and onboards
