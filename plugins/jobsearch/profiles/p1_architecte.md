# P1 — AI Architect / Solution Architect

**Definition**: Design the technical solution that answers the client's need — solution design, tech selection, GenAI architecture. Client-facing, not commercial. The core profile (~16 applications).

## Valid cells: all 5 company types

- P1×T1 — Industrial startup (Terabase, Overstory)
- P1×T2 — Engineering / BET (Egis, EP2C, Natural Power)
- P1×T3 — ESN / Consulting (ILLUIN, WEnvision, Le Hibou)
- P1×T4 — Large energy / industry group (TotalEnergies, Technip, Hilti, ASN, EDF)
- P1×T5 — AI SaaS / Lab (Cohere, Anthropic Applied AI, Tomorro, Mapbox)

## Narrative rules

**Lead experience**: Blue Green first (architectures delivered: EnBW/Valorem + BWC in prod) → Open Ocean (SaaS scaled) → Artelia (enterprise client understanding).

**Never hide technical depth**: the architect profile is credible precisely because it ships.

**T4 anti-pattern**: NEVER open on "founder" for large groups. Open on Artelia (London, international group, P&L) + major enterprise clients (EnBW, Valorem).

**T5 anti-pattern**: Solopreneur gap cost Renaud the Anthropic application explicitly. Emphasise: teams led (12 at Open Ocean), enterprise clients, not just solo builds.

## Solopreneur neutralisation

- Blue Green = "AI studio" with named enterprise clients (EnBW France, Valorem, IC Ingénieurs)
- Open Ocean in front when target is structured: team of 12, €2M raised, industrial exit (Artelia), clients DCNS/SBM Offshore/RTE
- Artelia = corporate proof: 3 years in London in an international engineering group, P&L, C-level
- Always a product in production as proof: BlueWind Companion v2.2 live

## Key differentiators

- Technical depth AND business communication
- 15+ years in energy/engineering = speaks the client's language
- Production GenAI (not POC): BWC v2.2, RAG 22,800 chunks, 5 Pydantic AI agents
