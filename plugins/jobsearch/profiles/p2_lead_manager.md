# P2 — AI Lead / Manager

**Definition**: AI team management, AI product delivery, structuring the AI function (~11 applications).

## Valid cells: 4 company types (no T2)

- P2×T1 — Industrial startup (Fuse Energy, Terabase)
- P2×T3 — ESN / Consulting (Médiane, Skapa, Quotacom)
- P2×T4 — Large energy / industry group (Tarkett AI Leader, Thales)
- P2×T5 — AI SaaS editor (iAdvize, JobTeaser, Mistral PM Science)

## Narrative rules

**Management proof comes from Open Ocean**: 12-person multidisciplinary team (engineers, data scientists, web), 8 years. This is the primary management credential.

**Blue Green shows technical currency**: the lead hasn't lost hands-on ability. Still ships production GenAI (RAG, agents) today.

**Artelia = cross-functional P&L**: corporate credibility — managed in a structured international environment.

**T4 anti-pattern**: Same as P1×T4 — open corporate (Artelia/group experience), not founder.

**T5 anti-pattern (iAdvize gap)**: Show appetite to integrate into an existing product org. Frame Open Ocean as "building inside a team", not "solo founder". Emphasise: roadmap, agile delivery, team, not independence.

## Solopreneur neutralisation

- Open Ocean: "12-person multidisciplinary product team" — hiring, roadmap, tech debt, investors
- Blue Green: "AI studio" not "consultant", multi-client delivery
- Artelia: corporate proof, cross-functional team management, P&L London

## Key differentiators

- Has lived scale-up from the inside: knows when to trade speed vs quality
- Stays technically hands-on: can still architect, still ships code
- Domain expertise (energy/industrial): can talk to business leadership AND engineering
